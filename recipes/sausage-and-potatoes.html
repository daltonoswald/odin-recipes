Skillet Sausage and Potatoes

A quick and delicious Sausage and Potatoes meal made in one skillet!

If you're looking for a quick and easy dinner with easy clean up, you've come to the right place!

This meal is very versatile and can be made on a budget, on a busy weekday night, and can be eaten for dinner or even breakfast! The recipe is easy to adjust so get creative with it!

    Ingredients
  • 1 pound small gold potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
  • 12 ounce fully-cooked smoked beef sausage, sliced 1/2 inch think
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced vertically
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced vertically
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1/2-inch chunks
  • 1 golden delicious apple - peeled, cored, and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup frozen petite peas
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • salt and freshlyu ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional)
    Steps
  1. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with salt water, bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until fork tender, 10-12 minutes, drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir smoke sausage in skillet until browned on both sides, about 3 minutes. Remove sausage from skillet to a paper towel-lined plate; leave drippings in pan.
  3. Quarter potatoes, cook and stir potatoes, red pepper, green pepper, onion, and apple in the skillet until potatoes are starting to brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Add oil as needed, stir in garlic; cook until fragrant, about 30 minutes.
  4. Return sausage to the skillet, stir in peas and thyme, and cook until pears are heated, 3 to 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper; garnish with chopped parsley.
